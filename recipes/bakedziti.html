Baked Ziti

What’s in Easy Baked Ziti

This baked pasta is so simple. There’s just a few ingredients (pasta, sauce, cheese, check!) and a few steps between you and a creamy, cheesy dinner!

Recipe Ingredients:

  • Ziti Pasta
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cream Cheese
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 375°F and spray an 11×9-inch casserole dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. In a large saucepan, cook the pasta according to package instructions (al dente/no salt), and drain. 1 pound uncooked ziti
  3. In the same large saucepan set over medium heat, cook the marinara sauce and cream cheese together until the cheese melts. Stir often. 1 32 ounce jar Marinara sauce,8 ounces cream cheese
  4. Add the pasta to the marinara/cheese sauce and mix well.
  5. Pour half the marinara/cheese/ziti sauce in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle with half the mozzarella and half the Parmesan cheeses. 16 ounces shredded mozzarella,½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  6. Sprinkle the cheeses with 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil. ¼ cup chopped fresh basil
  7. Layer the remaining marinara/cheese/ziti sauce over the cheese and top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  8. Cover and bake at 375° for 30 minutes.
  9. Remove the cover and bake an additional 15-20 minutes or until the baked ziti is bubbly and cheese is golden.
  10. Garnish with the remaining chopped basil and serve with toasted baguette.
  11. Enjoy!
